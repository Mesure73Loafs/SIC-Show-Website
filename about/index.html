What is the SIC Show?

The SIC Show is a technology podcast ran by Sam and Ian. They talk about tech news and discuss tech-related topics. Each of their episodes are full of fun and laziness. Join them every week on their Twitch page to watch the new episodes live, or go to either their YouTube page or listen to them on your favorite podcast services.



Who is on the SIC Show?

Ian and Sam are the SIC Show hosts. They do all of the work on their own. Sam does most of the research and talking, and Ian does most of the tech work, like producing and coding this website.



Why did Ian and Sam start the SIC Show?

Sam asked Ian if he wanted to start a podcast like the WAN Show, and they started it.



Why is it called the SIC Show?

It was originally called the Sam Ian Cedric Show, but after Cedric left, Ian and Sam renamed it to the Sam and Ian Criticism show.
